Code Mystique

Date: 5th February 2024 Time: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM Location: Room No-N505, SOC Academic Block, MIT ADT

Code Mystique was a fun and creative on-ground event filled with mystery and Bollywood clues. Students and faculty participated in a thrilling game where they were challenged to guess Bollywood movie names based on cryptic hints.

Participants entered themed rooms with various clues – including emojis, visual puzzles, quotes, and sound bites – that led them to iconic Bollywood movie titles.

Laughter, surprise, and creativity made it a refreshing break from regular coding events and helped build a stronger community through gamified learning.
