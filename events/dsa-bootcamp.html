DSA Bootcamp 2.0

Date: 10th – 12th April 2024 Time: 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM Location: MIT ADT University, Pune

DSA Bootcamp 2.0 was a comprehensive 3-day session designed to help students master essential data structures and algorithms. Organized by the CodeChef MIT-ADT Chapter, it featured live demos, real-world coding problems, and peer-to-peer mentorship from senior club members.

With over 130 enthusiastic participants, the sessions built a solid foundation for those preparing for placements and competitive programming.

Topics Covered (With Explanations & References)

  • Time & Space Complexity: Big O, Omega, Theta analysis. [Learn More]
  • Arrays & Strings: Traversal, sliding window, two pointers. [Learn More]
  • Linked Lists: Single, double, circular, reversal. [Learn More]
  • Stacks & Queues: Using arrays/linked lists, applications. [Learn More]
  • Recursion & Backtracking: Combinations, subsets, sudoku solver. [Learn More]
  • Sorting Algorithms: Merge, Quick, Heap sort. [Learn More]
  • Binary Search: Standard problems and variations. [Learn More]
  • Trees: Binary Trees, BST, traversal techniques. [Learn More]
  • Graphs: BFS, DFS, adjacency list, shortest path. [Learn More]
  • Dynamic Programming: Tabulation, memoization techniques. [Learn More]
  • Greedy Algorithms: Activity selection, coin change. [Learn More]
  • Practice Problems: From LeetCode, CodeChef, and HackerRank. [Practice]

Speakers & Mentors

  • Swarnadeep Barik - Vice President, taught Linked Lists, Trees & DP.
  • Nikhil Parbat - Technical Lead, handled Graphs, Stacks & Queues.
  • Kartikeyan Anand - President, taught Sorting, Searching & Complexity Analysis.
  • Reet Kaur Nahal - Secretary, taught Recursion & Backtracking.
  • Guest Mentor: Abhay Babbar (SDE @ Lattice Semi) gave a session on competitive problem-solving with Greedy & DP.
