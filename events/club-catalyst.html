Club Catalyst

Date: 26th February 2025 Time: 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM Location: MIT ADT University, Design Lawn

Club Catalyst was a grand introduction of the CodeChef MIT-ADT Chapter to the first-year students. It served as a window into the diverse activities, achievements, and opportunities our club offers to the coding community at MIT ADT.

We highlighted our mission of empowering students through coding, development, and community-driven peer learning. From competitive programming to real-world tech discussions, we support curious minds with the resources and mentorship they need.

What We Do

  • Weekly Coding Quizes: Focused on Data Structures, Algorithms, and CP platforms like CodeChef, Codeforces, and LeetCode.
  • Real-World Development: Web dev, AI/ML projects, and open-source contributions guided by experienced mentors.
  • Tech Talks & Events: Sessions with alumni and industry experts to bridge the campus-to-corporate gap.
  • Hackathons & Competitions: Organizing and participating in inter-college and national events.

Our Past Events

  • CPP Bootcamp 2.0: Hands-on 3-day workshop on C++ programming fundamentals and advanced concepts.
  • DSA Bootcamp: Structured learning on data structures and algorithms with problem-solving sessions.
  • ValiCQ 2.0: A three-round team-based coding competition—Quiz, Debugging, and Coding—concluded with top 3 winning teams.
  • Chai with Alumni: Insights from 7 successful alumni who guided students on navigating the tech industry.
  • Code Mystique: An offline, mystery Bollywood-themed coding game held at Persona Fest.
