About Us

CodeChef MIT-ADT is one of the oldest and most prominent coding clubs at MITADT, dedicated to nurturing a strong culture of competitive programming, problem-solving, and data structures & algorithms (DSA). We regularly host workshops, coding contests, and hackathons to empower students in sharpening their coding skills

Our mission is to create a community of passionate coders who can learn, and grow together in the field of computer science and technology.
